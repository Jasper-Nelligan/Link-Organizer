Adding your Google Calendar

If you use Google Calendar you can add an interactive version of it right here on this page!

With this you'll be able to see all your upcoming events and due dates. Adding new events must be done using the actual Google Calendar website.

You'll have to fetch your Google Calendar's public URL in order to display it here. Here's the steps required to find your public URL:

1. On your computer, open Google Calendar.
2. In the top right, click on the gear icon to open up settings.
3. On the left, under "Setting for my calendars", click on the calendar you want to add. A dropdown menu should appear. From the dropdown menu, click "Integrate calendar".
4. Copy the link under "Public URL for my calendar" and paste it below!
